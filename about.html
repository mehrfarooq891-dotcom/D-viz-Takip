About DövizTakip

Free. Instant. No signup required.

What We Do

DövizTakip is a dedicated financial tool designed to provide real-time currency exchange rates and advanced inflation calculation for users in Turkey. We help investors, travelers, and citizens track the purchasing power of the Turkish Lira across different economic cycles. Our platform is completely free to use and provides transparent, instant data without any barriers.

Why We Built This

Most financial tools are locked behind paywalls or require creating an account just to see a number. We believe useful tools should be free and instant for everyone. In a fast-moving economy like Turkey's, having access to clear currency trends and inflation metrics is a necessity, not a luxury.

100% Free

No hidden fees, no subscriptions, ever. Our platform is supported by community use and remains accessible to all.

No Signup

No account needed. We don't ask for your email or personal details. Your data stays private and your experience stays fast.

Built Local

Designed for real conditions, local currency (TRY), and local economic metrics of Turkey. We understand the specific needs of our users.

DövizTakip is a free online currency tracker and inflation calculator for Turkey. Use it anytime with no registration required.
